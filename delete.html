Account Deletion Policy

If you still decide to delete your Vobee account after careful consideration, please read carefully and fully understand this policy. After accepting all its content, please proceed with the next step according to our operation guidelines for deletion.

By starting a deletion through the deletion method provided in Part 3 of this policy, you are deemed to have accepted to sign and abide by the entire content of this policy.

If you need to use our services again after deletion, you are welcome to re-register using your mobile phone number or other methods allowed by Vobee. At the same time, we would also love to hear about your experience with our products/services. You can contact us at Support@Vobeefun.com so that we can further improve and perfect our services.
